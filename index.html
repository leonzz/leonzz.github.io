Currently I'm working in GCP (Google Cloud Platform) at Google's Waterloo office.


This is my previous page when I worked as a research/software engineer in the Ryerson Multimedia Research Laboratory.


I received my Master of Applied Science degree in Electrical and Computer Engineering in 2014, under the supervision of Dr. Ling Guan, and Dr. Tim McInerney, with a focus on research and development of HCI (Human Computer Interface), Augmented Reality and Virtual Reality system and applications.
